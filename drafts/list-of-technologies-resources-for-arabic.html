OCR, digitization, etc.

This space is broad with digitization efforts strongly represented by:

Maktaba Shamela OpenITI Dragomen - my own project

Open Source Arabic OCR:

Kraken - commandline escriptorium - web-based complete solution

Harakat, Tarkeeb (linguistic annotation), etc.

Adding diacritics:

shakkelha - the best current solution, OSS haracat - was buggy and did not work, OSS

Tarkeeb:

INCEpTION - OSS Quran corpus - partially completed, not OSS (except data)

Dictionaries

aratools Arabic Student's Dictionary Alpheios - browser plugin, OSS Fuzzy Arabic Dictionary - use transliterated words without knowing spelling, OSS https://cooljugator.com/ar - finding verb conjugations [https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/])https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/) arabdict - multi-language Almaany - multi-language ArabicReference - Hans Wehr equivalent
