Who are the successful people?

Posted on Sun 10 April 2022 in Islam

بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

The Quran and Ahadith contain many references to people who are considered successful. The primary goal of this article is to accumulate and summarize who the successful people are, with references to the various verses in the Quran and Ahadith.

The inspiration for documenting this is from the dua(supplication) Muslims read after performing the wudhu(ablution):

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنِي مِنَ التَّوَّابِينَ وَاجْعَلْنِي مِنَ الْمُتَطَهِّرِينَ
"O Allah, make me among those who repent, and make me among those who purify themselves."

those who repent - التَّوَّابِين
those who purify themselves - الْمُتَطَهِّرِين
(dua after wudhu)
"... Indeed, Allāh loves those who are constantly repentant and loves those who purify themselves." (Quran 2:222)

those who rely upon Him - الْمُتَوَكِّلِينَ
"... Indeed, Allāh loves those who rely (upon Him)." (Quran 3:159)

those who are successful - الْمُفْلِحُونَ
"... and it is those who are the successful." (Quran 2:5)

those who are God-fearing (or have taqwa) - الْمُتَّقِينَ
"... then, Allah loves the God-fearing." (Quran 3:76)

the righteous - الصَّالِحِينَ or الصَّالِحُونَ
"... And those are among the righteous." (Quran 3:114)

the truthful - الصِّدِّيقِينَ or الصَّادِقِينَ
"And whoever obeys Allāh and the Messenger - those will be with the ones upon whom Allāh has bestowed favor of the prophets, the steadfast affirmers of truth, the martyrs and the righteous." (Quran 4:69)

the martyrs - الشُّهَدَاءِ
(see translation above) (Quran 4:69)

the patient - الصَّابِرِينَ
"...Indeed, Allāh is with the patient."(Quran 2:153)

those who spend [in the way of Allāh] - الْمُنفِقِينَ
"...The patient, the true, the obedient, those who spend [in the way of Allāh], and those who seek forgiveness before dawn." (Quran 3:17)

those who seek forgiveness before dawn - الْمُسْتَغْفِرِينَ
(see translation above) (Quran 3:17)

the obedient - الْقَانِتِينَ
"Indeed, the Muslim men and Muslim women, the believing men and believing women, the obedient men and obedient women, the truthful men and truthful women, the patient men and patient women, the humble men and humble women, the charitable men and charitable women, the fasting men and fasting women, the men who guard their private parts and the women who do so, and the men who remember Allāh often and the women who do so - for them Allāh has prepared forgiveness and a great reward." (Quran 33:35)

the Muslims - الْمُسْلِمِينَ
(see translation above) (Quran 33:35)

the believers - الْمُؤْمِنِينَ
(see translation above) (Quran 33:35)

the humble - الْخَاشِعِينَ
(see translation above) (Quran 33:35)

the charitable - الْمُتَصَدِّقِينَ
(see translation above) (Quran 33:35)

the fasting - الصَّائِمِينَ
(see translation above) (Quran 33:35)

the ones who guard their private parts - الْحَافِظِينَ فُرُوجَهُمْ
(see translation above) (Quran 33:35)

the ones who remember Allāh often - الذَّاكِرِينَ اللَّهَ كَثِيرًا
(see translation above) (Quran 33:35)

the forerunners/foremost - السَّابِقُونَ
"And the forerunners, the forerunners." (Quran 56:10)
