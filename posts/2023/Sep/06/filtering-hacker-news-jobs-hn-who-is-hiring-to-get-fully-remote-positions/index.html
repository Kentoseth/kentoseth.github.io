A list of keywords to use for filtering out partially remote jobs on HNHiring, which is a scraper of job posts of the monthly Hacker News "Who is hiring?"

An alternative job option is: Y Combinator's Work at a Startup

What you need

  • US Remote
  • Remote in
  • US only
  • EU only
  • Remote ()
  • Remote US
  • Remote EU
  • USA / Canada / EU / UK
  • preferred
  • or Remote
  • Remote US
  • Remote EU
