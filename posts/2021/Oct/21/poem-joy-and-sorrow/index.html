Poem: Joy and Sorrow

Posted on Thu 21 October 2021 in Islam

As I decay, I watch the world full of joy.

Laughter, pleasure and fun all around, The orphan, widow and pauper nowhere to be found.

The suffering ones are tucked away, Hidden beneath an extravagant world gone astray.

Who Hears their suffering except He? There is a moment where they will find glee.

Their souls will be dipped for a moment in Heaven, And they will respond: "Suffering? We cannot remember"

And this is the world that is temporary, If you remember this, you are free.
